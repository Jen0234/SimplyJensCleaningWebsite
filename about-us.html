OUR STORY

How Simply Jens Cleaning Began

Simply Jens Cleaning was founded by Jenna and Jennifer with one simple goal: to help people enjoy cleaner homes and less stressful lives.

We understand how busy life can become. Between work, family, appointments, and everyday responsibilities, keeping up with cleaning isn't always easy. That's why we're committed to providing dependable cleaning services that give our clients more time to focus on what matters most.

Today, we're proud to serve Ottawa, Gatineau, and surrounding communities, offering residential, commercial, housekeeping, deep cleaning, move-in / move-out cleaning, and customized cleaning solutions.
