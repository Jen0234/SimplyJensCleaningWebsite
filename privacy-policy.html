Privacy Policy

At Simply Jens Cleaning, your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information.

Last Updated: July 2026

1. Information We Collect

We may collect your full name, phone number, email address, property address, cleaning service requested, appointment details, uploaded photos, and messages submitted through our website forms.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to respond to quote requests, schedule appointments, provide cleaning services, send invoices and receipts, communicate about appointments, respond to questions or concerns, and improve our website and customer experience.

3. Photos & File Uploads

If you upload photos through our website, they are used to assess cleaning needs, prepare estimates, or better understand concerns. Photos are not used for marketing unless you give us permission.

4. Google Analytics & Cookies

Our website may use Google Analytics and cookies to understand how visitors use our website. This may include anonymous data such as pages viewed, device type, browser type, and time spent on the website.

5. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell or rent your personal information. We may share information only when necessary with trusted service providers such as booking software, payment processors, website hosting providers, or when required by law.

6. Data Security

We take reasonable precautions to protect your personal information. However, no online system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Your Rights

You may request access to your personal information, ask us to correct inaccurate information, update your contact details, or request deletion where permitted by law.

8. Third-Party Links

Our website may link to third-party websites such as Square, Google Reviews, Facebook, Instagram, and other platforms. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those websites.

9. Children’s Privacy

Our website is not intended for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with the revised date.
