Accessibility Statement

Simply Jens Cleaning is committed to providing an accessible website experience for all visitors, regardless of ability or technology.

Last Updated: July 2026

1. Our Commitment

At Simply Jens Cleaning, we believe everyone should be able to access information about our services. We are committed to making our website as accessible and user-friendly as possible for all visitors.

2. Accessibility Features

We work to provide an accessible experience by using clear navigation, readable fonts, descriptive headings, responsive layouts, and content that can be viewed across a variety of devices and screen sizes.

3. Ongoing Improvements

Accessibility is an ongoing process. We regularly review our website and continue making improvements to provide a better experience for all users.

4. Third-Party Services

Some features on our website may rely on third-party services such as booking platforms, maps, payment processors, or social media. While we strive to use accessible services, we cannot guarantee the accessibility of third-party websites or applications.

5. Need Assistance?

If you experience difficulty using our website or need information in another format, please contact us. We'll do our best to assist you and provide the information you need.

6. Feedback

We welcome feedback regarding the accessibility of our website. Your comments help us improve the experience for all visitors.
