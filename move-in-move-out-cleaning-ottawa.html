Kitchen Cleaning

Countertops, sinks, cabinets, floors, and all accessible appliances included.

Bathroom Sanitizing

Toilets, sinks, tubs, showers, mirrors, fixtures, and floors cleaned and sanitized.

Inside Cabinets & Drawers

Empty cabinets, drawers, closets, and shelves wiped inside and out.

Baseboards & Details

Baseboards, doors, trim, switches, handles, and high-touch areas cleaned.

Interior Windows
