Why Homeowners in Ottawa Choose Simply Jens Cleaning

Simply Jens Cleaning was built on one simple belief — your home deserves consistent, thoughtful care. We understand that inviting someone into your home requires trust. That’s why we focus on reliability, respect, and attention to detail in every visit. Whether it’s a busy family household or a quiet, pet-friendly space, we clean with care and professionalism. As a locally operated business serving Ottawa and surrounding areas, we’re committed to providing transparent pricing, flexible scheduling, and dependable service you can feel comfortable relying on. Our goal isn’t just to clean — it’s to create a refreshed, stress-free environment you can truly enjoy.
